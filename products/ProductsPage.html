Indie

Origin: India
Average Lifespan: 12-16 years
Size: Medium; 18-24 inches tall, 25-45 pounds
Diet: Omnivorous; balanced diet of meat, vegetables, and grains
Area: Urban and rural areas in India
Characteristics: Intelligent, alert, and low-maintenance

Swiss Mountain Dog

Origin: Switzerland
Average Lifespan: 8-11 years
Size: Large; males 25.5-28.5 inches tall, 115-140 pounds; females 23.5-27 inches tall, 85-110 pounds
Diet: High-quality dog food, preferably formulated for large breeds
Area: Prefers cooler climates; originally bred for the Swiss Alps
Characteristics: Strong, dependable, and family-oriented

Border Collie

Origin: Border region between England and Scotland
Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
Size: Medium; 18-22 inches tall, 30-55 pounds
Diet: High-energy diet; balanced mix of proteins, fats, and carbohydrates
Area: Best suited for rural or suburban areas with plenty of space to run
Characteristics: Highly intelligent, energetic, and trainable

Golden Retriever

Origin: Scotland
Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
Size: Medium; males 23-24 inches tall, 65-75 pounds; females 21.5-22.5 inches tall, 55-65 pounds
Diet: High-quality dog food, balanced diet
Area: Adaptable to various environments, enjoys outdoor activities
Characteristics: Friendly, intelligent, and devoted. Excellent family pets and service dogs

German Shepherd

Origin: Germany
Average Lifespan: 9-13 years
Size: Large; males 24-26 inches tall, 65-90 pounds; females 22-24 inches tall, 50-70 pounds
Diet: High-quality dog food, balanced diet
Area: Adaptable, but needs space for exercise
Characteristics: Intelligent, loyal, and versatile. Often used in police and military roles
